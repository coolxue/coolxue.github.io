  • Welcome to CoolXue Learning Center

    CoolXue Learning Center is an innovative, engaging school, located in Richmond Hill, offering a balanced curriculum for secondary school students. Our goal is to challenge each student to reach her or his maximum potential and to equip each student with a set of skills and values that will enable each, as individuals, to attain both future personal and academic success.

  • Vision and Values

    CoolXue Learning Center aims to make students be happy, confident and well rounded citizens. The following values guide our thoughts and actions each day: 1) provide learning opportunities that deliver the highest standards of achievement; 2) provide students with a nurturing, challenging and fun curriculum; 3) place great emphasis on developing skills in math, physics and computers.
