  • Welcome to CoolXue Education

    CoolXue Education is an innovative, engaging school, located in Richmond Hill, offering a balanced curriculum for secondary school students. Our goal is to challenge each student to reach her or his maximum potential and to equip each student with a set of skills and values that will enable each, as individuals, to attain both future personal and academic success.

  • Vision and Values

    CoolXue Education aims to make students be happy, confident and well rounded citizens. The following values guide our thoughts and actions each day: 1) we provide learning opportunities that deliver the highest standards of achievement; 2) we provide students with a nurturing, challenging and fun curriculum; 3) we place great emphasis on developing skills in math, physics and computers.

  • Education is the key to success

    Education opens doors for people of all backgrounds, and it expands the human mind with knowledge. At CoolXue, we teach your child at an early stage through a challenging learn and practice methodology that enables them to master Math, Physics and Computer skills.
