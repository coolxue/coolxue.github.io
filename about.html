About Us

CoolXue Education is an innovative and dedicated school located in Richmond Hill that aims to change the way students learn and acquire life skills including self-confidence, communication, collaboration and essential social skills.

In CoolXue, we take small group class. We strongly believe that hands-on small group classroom training is the fun and effective way to learn.

CoolXue is founded by a team of skilled, dedicated, professional teachers. We carefully selects and designs every course aimed at helping students reach their full potential.

Each student has individual talents. Our experienced teachers get to know each student through an in-person meeting and assessment before the start of class.

We develop the ideal learning curve plan for students. This is followed by a patient explanation of each course objective to help the student to complete every homework independently.

Education is the key to success.

In CoolXue, our dream is to unlock every student's potential by an excellent education curriculum that complements Ontario curriculum and is delivered by highly skilled professional teachers.
